3.4 Using Pipes

Exercise 3b

Read the instructions for printing in the computer science labs, at http://www.uml.edu/Sciences/computer-science/Faq/Printing.aspx , and then return here.

Using pipes, print all lines of list1 and list2 containing the letter 'p', sort the result, and print to the printer HP_BW_NORTH. Retrieve the printout in your lab room.

 

Answer

$ cat list1 list2 | grep p | sort | lpr -P HP_BW_NORTH -U your_student_id#
